## Super Mario 64

![A screenshot of Super Mario 64](<../.gitbook/assets/image (2) (1).png>)

### Overview

Super Mario 64, released in 1996, is a 3d platforming game developed by Nintendo for the Nintendo 64. The player controls Mario through various courses filled with obstacles, rewards and enemies. Mario must navigate through these worlds in order to save Princess Peach from Bowser's Castle.

### Movement

Super Mario 64 has more controls and features of movement than any of the previous Mario franchise instalments. In the game, Mario can walk, run, jump, crouch, crawl, climb, swim, kick, grab objects, and punch all through the use of various joysticks and buttons on the Nintendo 64 controller(<img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (1).png" alt="" data-size="line">).
